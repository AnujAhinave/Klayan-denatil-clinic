Understand Your Treatment

Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

We specialize in **Single-Sitting RCT**. Using digital apex locators and rotary endodontics, Dr. Sabina removes infected pulp with microscopic precision, saving your natural tooth from extraction in just 45-60 minutes.

Wisdom Tooth Surgery

Impacted wisdom teeth can cause severe pain and shifting. Our **Atraumatic Extraction** technique ensures minimal tissue trauma, leading to faster healing and virtually no post-operative swelling.
